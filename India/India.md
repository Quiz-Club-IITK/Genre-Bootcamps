### Minority Religions

**Jainism**
- Tirthankaras: Rishabhanatha, Parshvanatha, Mahavira
- Bahubali
- Digambara vs Shvetambara
- Temples: Dilwara, Ranakpur, Palitana, Shravanabelagola
- Concepts: Ahimsa, Anekantavada, Syadvada
- Texts: Agamas, Tattvartha Sutra

**Sikhism**
- Ten Gurus
- Guru Granth Sahib
- Harmandir Sahib & Five Takhts
- Khalsa: Panj Pyare, Five Ks
- Sikh Empire: Misls, Ranjit Singh
- Anglo-Sikh Wars, Operation Blue Star

**Buddhism**
- Buddha's life: Lumbini, Bodh Gaya, Sarnath, Kushinagar
- Schools: Theravada, Mahayana, Vajrayana
- Four Noble Truths, Eightfold Path, Nirvana
- Sites: Nalanda, Sanchi
- Texts: Tripitaka, Dhammapada
- Ambedkar & Navayana Buddhism

**Parsis**

- Ahura Mazda, Avesta
- Fire temples: Udvada
- Notable figures: Tata family, Dadabhai Naoroji, Homi Bhabha, Freddie Mercury

**Judaism**

- Paradesi Synagogue
- Notable figures: Sassoon family, J. F. R. Jacob

**Christianity**
- St Thomas tradition
- Syrian Christians
- Catholic vs Protestant
- Se Cathedral, Goa
- Notable figures: Mother Teresa, K. R. Narayanan
